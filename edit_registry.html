My son recently received this as a gift for his first birthday, and it is one of his favorite presents! The Sub is very lightweight and easy to maneuver, but bulky and durable at the same time. When you fill it with water, the front acts as a spout and is a great "rinser". If you tip it to the side, the water flows out of the four portholes like a waterfall. My son also loves to spin the propeller on the back. Great toy for a 1 yr old, and up. Highly recommend!
