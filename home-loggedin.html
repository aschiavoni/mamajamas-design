Need help setting up your nursery?

Mamajamas gives you a personalized list of baby gear to customize and share with other parents.

The idea for Mamajamas was born out of my own experience as an expecting mom three years ago.

I was overwhelmed by the process of figuring out what to get in preparation. I spent hours researching products online and emailed mom friends asking for advice. They sent me long spreadsheets. After sifting through everything, I had to then create my own “master” spreadsheet to keep myself organized.

I starting thinking there must be an easier way to get this kind of information.

Meanwhile, I found out that many of my new mom friends were having the same experience. I also learned that the long process of figuring out what to get doesn’t end when the baby is born. At each new phase of their development you need new things.

The goal of Mamajamas is to help new and expecting parents buy only stuff they need — to help them spend less time thinking about stuff and more time enjoying pregnancy and new parenthood.

I hope Mamajamas is helpful to you during this exciting time!


Angie Schiavoni
Founder
