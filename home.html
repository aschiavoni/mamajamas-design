The idea for Mamajamas was born out of my own experience as an expecting mom four years ago.

I was overwhelmed by the process of figuring out what I needed to prepare for the birth of my son. I also wondered how I could help friends and family get me things I’d need for the baby, when I didn’t even know myself! I spent hours researching products online and emailing mom friends asking for advice.

I started thinking that there should be an easier way for expecting parents to build their registries and get baby gear recommendations. I wanted someone to tell me what I would need (and wouldn’t!) based on how I was planning to parent, and I wanted an easy way to get recommendations from my new parent friends.

The goal of Mamajamas is to take all of the guesswork out of building your baby registry. I want you to be able to spend less time thinking about stuff, and more time enjoying pregnancy and new parenthood.

I hope your Mamajamas baby registry is helpful to you during this exciting time!


Angie Schiavoni, Founder
