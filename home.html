The Mamajamas birth story

The idea for Mamajamas was born out of my own experience as an expecting mom.

Four years ago, I was overwhelmed trying to figure out what I needed for the birth of my son. How could I help my family and friends buy us things when I didn't even know what I needed myself? I spent hours researching products online and emailing mom friends asking for advice.

I realized there needed to be an easier way for expecting parents to build their registries and get gear recommendations. I wanted to find like-minded parents and find out what worked for them (and what didn’t!).

For ideas, you can start by checking out my registry here: www.mamajamas.com/angie

The goal of Mamajamas is to take all of the guesswork out of building your baby registry. I want you to spend less time thinking about stuff and more time enjoying pregnancy and parenthood.

Congratulations and welcome!


Angie Schiavoni, Founder
