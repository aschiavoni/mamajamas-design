Towel

Pottery Barn Kids Hooded Towel

Add
I got a bunch of different towels as baby gifts. I liked the pottery barn kids one the best although, if I was buying them for myself, I don't think I would spend $23.00 on a baby towel. Plus, by around 6 to 8 months they can use regular bath towels no problem.
Pre-birth
2
Bath Tub

The First Year's Infant To Toddler Tub with Sling

Add
For the first few weeks I bathed my son in the bathroom sink with one of those cheap little sponges you can buy for the bottom of the sink. But then I got this tub as he grew out of the sink, and I wish I had gotten it sooner. It holds them securely and makes bathing them easy.

Shampoo or Body Wash

Avalon Gentle Tear-Free Baby Shampoo

Add
This is my current favorite. It's not greasy with a light, pleasant smell. I also really like the Weleda and California Baby brands. I didn't use soap on my baby for the first month. Even now, I only use it once every 3 days (or if he is looking especially grimey).
4-6 mo
2
Rinse Cup or Visor

Munchkin Shampoo Rinser

Add
Having a rinse visor is nice because less shampoo will get into your baby's eyes. I'm not in love with this specific item, but think in general getting one is a good idea.
4-6 mo

Bath Toy

Draw In The Tub Crayons

Add
I hear it's pretty common for toddlers to go through a bath strike aroudn 15-18 months. My son sure did. But then I ordered these bath crayons, and problem solved! He was excited to take a bath again because he got to draw. It's a little bit of a pain to always have to wipe down the bath after each use, but it's totally worth it for how much fun he has using these crayons in the bath!

Value shopper
Tags: Urban

I have a 2 1/2 year old son. I gravitate towards organic and wholesome parenting type products. I am willing to spend a little more for quality, but have a hard time spending a lot unless I'm really excited about something. I am also a huge fan of finding great baby gear at consignment or thrift stores (especially clothes!) and love a good bargain. I also have a slight addiction to amazon.com. I order way too many items late night on my phone (do not get the amazon iphone app unless you are ready to impulse buy!).
