Must-Have

Bath Toy

#4: Filled, expandable note

Add
My rating:
Buy at 4-6 months
Needs 1 of these
Gift this to Angie

Review/comment


My son recently received this as a gift for his first birthday, and it is one of his favorite presents! The Sub is very lightweight and easy to maneuver, but bulky and durable at the same time. When you fill it with water, the front acts as a spout and is a great "rinser". If you tip it to the side, the water flows out of the four portholes like a waterfall. My son also loves to spin the propeller on the back. Great toy for a 1 yr old, and up. Highly recommend!

Consider

Bath Tub

Fisher-Price Precious Planet Whale of a Tub

Add
My rating:
Buy pre-birth
Has 1 of these

Save yourself the time and energy looking for a baby bath tub and head straight for this one. I'm so happy with this tub that I find myself making more time for bath time with my baby!

Shampoo or Body Wash

Aveeno Baby Wash & Shampoo, 18 Ounce

Add
My rating:
Buy at 0–3 months
Needs 1 of these
Gift this to Angie

A lot of Aveeno products are great for the sensitive skin aspect. This one is iffy. It's a good product but if your baby is extra sensitive, it could make them break out. I've avoided that with my baby because I use a very, VERY small amount each time. If I don't, he does get a little red and I've got to be very cautious with him. But the right amount and that problem is eliminated.

LifeSafer Inflatable Neck Ring

My rating:

I have one of these.

Received as a gift from someone who obviously does not have kids. The safety of the design needs a re-think.
